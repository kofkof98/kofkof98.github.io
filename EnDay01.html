國營英文高效準備：Day 01

單字 A 系列、相似詞比較、搭配詞精準掌握

核心字彙與教材例句

1. above (adj./adv./prep.) 上面的；在...之上

There is a bird **above** the tree. (小鳥在樹的正上面。)

2. absence (n.) 缺席

Kevin got COVID yesterday, so he was **absent** for the class. (Kevin 昨天確診新冠病毒，所以課沒來上。)

補：**absent** (adj.) 缺席的

3. abstain (vi.) 避免；避開

使用上後面加 **from**

You have to **abstain from** eating fried chicken and bread in order to stay in shape. (為了保持體態，你應該避免吃炸雞或麵包。)

4. abundant (adj.) 充足的；豐富的

There is **abundant** evidence that nuclear power plants have harmful effects on the environment. (有充分的證據顯示核電廠對環境有害。)

5. abroad (adv.) 在國外

If you want to go **abroad**, you should take the airplane. (如果你要出國，你應該要搭飛機。)

6. **absolutely** (adv.) 絕對地；完全地

We should trust him **absolutely**. (我們應當完全的相信他。)

7. absorb (v.) (尤指逐漸)吸收

Plants **absorb** carbon dioxide. (植物吸收二氧化碳。)

8. accommodate (vt.) 能容納；提供住宿

The hotel is big enough to **accommodate** three thousand people per day. (這飯店大到可以一天容納三千人。)

9. accumulate (vt./vi.) 累積；積聚

If you want to **accumulate** more wealth, you have to learn how to invest your money. (若你想累積更多財富，你要學習如何投資。)

10. account (vi./n.) 解釋；對...負責；佔有/帳戶

Kevin's fault **accounts for** 90% of the car accident. (Kevin 的肇責佔車禍的 90 趴。)

英文相似字比較（不容忽視的細節）

1. abroad 與 aboard

abroad 是「國外」；aboard 是「上（船、飛機等）」。

2. adapt、adopt 與 adept

adapt (適應)；adopt (採用/領養)；adept (內行的)。

3. affect、effect 與 affection

affect (v. 影響)；effect (n. 影響)；affection (感情)。

4. allusion、illusion 與 delusion

allusion (影射)；illusion (錯覺/幻覺)；delusion (錯覺/妄想)。

5. attribute 與 distribute

attribute (歸因於)；distribute (分配/分派)。

6. tribute

tribute (敬意/供品)；常見用法 pay tribute to (展現敬意)。

7. amend、emend 與 recommend

amend (修正)；emend (校正)；recommend (推薦)。

8. amoral 與 immoral

amoral (與道德無關的)；immoral (不道德的)。

9. angel、angle 與 rectangle

angel (天使)；angle (角度)；rectangle (長方形)。

10. ardor 與 adore

ardor (熱情)；adore (愛慕/崇拜)。

常見搭配詞與片語 (p. 58)

1. abide by (遵守)

People have to **abide by** the law.

2. above all (尤其重要的是)

**Above all**, don't drink and drive.

3. account for (解釋/負責/佔比)

Civil servants **account for** the majority.

4. accuse sb. of sth. (控告某人)

The police **accuse** the man **of** murder.

5. adhere to (堅持某信念)

She **adhered to** her principles.

6. add insult to injury (雪上加霜)

...and to **add insult to injury**...

7. adjust to (使適應/調整)

You can **adjust** the desk **to** suit you.

8. after all (畢竟)

**After all**, they are my siblings.

9. against one's will (違背意願)

Don't go **against your will**.

10. all ears (洗耳恭聽)

I'm **all ears**.
