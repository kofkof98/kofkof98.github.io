1. 單字字彙 (Vocabulary)

affect
vt. 影響；感動；感染
I think Roy is sincere and quite without affectation, which affects me a lot. 我覺得 Roy 很真誠且毫不做作，這影響我很大。
(補: affectation n. 做作；裝模作樣)
afterwards
adv. 之後；後來
Afterwards, he became the husband of the good-looking woman. 後來，他成為一位漂亮女人的老公。
affordable
adj. 負擔得起的
Nowadays, most of the computers are sold at affordable prices, which enables people to buy more computers. 當今，大部分電腦以可負擔的價格出售，這使得人們買更多台電腦。
agenda
n. [C] 議事日程；(會議的)議程
There were several important items on the agenda. 會議議程中有幾項重要的議題。
agent
n. [C] 代理人；代理商；仲介
You can get a plane ticket via a travel agent. 你可以透過旅行社購買一張飛機票。
(via = by means of 藉由)
agriculture
n. 農業
Yunlin is an agricultural county of Taiwan. 雲林是台灣的農業縣。
(補: agricultural adj. 農業的)
aggressive
adj. 好鬥的；侵略的
Every war is aggressive, we have to avoid the war from happening. 每場戰爭都是侵略性的，我們要避免戰爭發生。
agreement
n. 同意；協定
We hope that Ukraine and Russia can reach an agreement to stop the war. 我們希望烏克蘭與俄羅斯能達成協議來停止戰爭。
(reach an agreement 達成協議)
allergic
adj. 對...過敏的；對...厭惡的
The man is allergic to pollen. 這男人對花粉過敏。
allocate
vt. 分配；分派
The CEO allocates necessary resources for the production of the products. 執行長分配必要的資源給產品生產。

3. 搭配詞 (Collocations) - 範圍 p.61-63

請特別注意粗體字的介系詞或動詞搭配。

31. bring up
提出；養育
The student brought up a problem about English collocation. 學生提出了一個關於英文搭配詞的問題。
32. blow hot and cold
前後矛盾；態度搖擺不定
He's been blowing hot and cold about the trip to Japan. He changed the schedule every day. 他對日本之行一直前後矛盾。他每天都在改變行程。
33. build on sand
根基不穩 (建在沙子上)
Your English proficiency is building on sand. Please practice more in daily life. 你的英語水平根基不穩。請在日常生活中多加練習。
34. beat the air
白費力氣 (打空氣)
The movie is going to end in ten minutes. Don't beat the air to arrive there. 電影還有十分鐘就結束。不要白費力氣前往那裡。
35. be based on
以...為基礎；根據...改編
The opera is based on a novel. 這歌劇是根據小說改編的。
36. by all means
好的；當然；務必
Would you lend me some pencils? By all means! 你能借我一些鉛筆嗎？當然！
37. by no means
絕不；一點也不
Roy earns millions of dollars in a year. That is by no means a small accomplishment. Roy 一年賺了數百萬元。這絕不是一個小成就。
38. block out
阻擋；遮蔽
That tall building outside the window blocks out the sun. 窗外那座高樓遮擋了陽光。
39. come up with
想出 (點子、計畫)
My colleague came up with a great idea to solve the problems of our products. 我的同事想出了一個好主意來解決我們產品的問題。
40. come into being
產生；誕生
No one knows how the products came into being. 沒有人知道這些產品是如何產生的。
