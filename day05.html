1. 單字字彙 (Vocabulary)

allow
vt. 允許；准許
My boss allows me to take a personal leave for 3 days. 我的老闆允許我請三天的事假。
(allow sb. to V 允許某人做某事)
amateur
n. 業餘愛好者；adj. 業餘的
He is an amateur photographer. 他是一位業餘的攝影師。
allusive
adj. 暗指的；引經據典的
Her speech was very allusive; you had to know a lot of literature to understand it. 她的演講充滿了典故；你需要懂很多文學才能理解。
altitude
n. 高度；海拔
The plane is flying at an altitude of 20,000 feet. 飛機正在兩萬英呎的高空飛行。
amazing
adj. 令人驚奇的；驚人的
It is amazing that no one was hurt in the car accident. 很驚人的是，沒有人在那場車禍中受傷。
amend
vt. 修改；修訂 (法律、規則等)
The parliament will amend the law to protect animal rights. 議會將修訂法律以保護動物權利。
ambiguous
adj. 模稜兩可的；含糊不清的
The government's position on this issue is highly ambiguous. 政府在這個議題上的立場非常模稜兩可。
among
prep. 在...之中 (三者或以上)
The little girl sat among the guests, listening to their stories. 小女孩坐在客人中間，聽著他們的故事。
ambassador
n. 大使
He served as the US ambassador to Japan. 他曾擔任美國駐日本大使。
announce
vt. 宣布；宣告
They announced their engagement to the family. 他們向家人宣布了訂婚的消息。

3. 搭配詞 (Collocations) - 範圍 p.63-64

請特別注意粗體字的介系詞或動詞搭配。

41. consist of
包含；由...組成 (= be composed of)
The committee consists of ten members. 這個委員會由十名成員組成。
42. cope with
應付；處理
It must be difficult for her to cope with three children and a job. 對她來說，要應付三個孩子和一份工作一定很困難。
43. call for
需要；呼籲
This situation calls for immediate action. 這個情況需要立即採取行動。
44. call up
打電話給...；徵召(入伍)；使想起
He was called up for military service when the war began. 戰爭開始時他被徵召入伍。
45. cry wolf
發假警報；說謊 (放羊的孩子)
If you cry wolf too often, people will stop believing you. 如果你太常放假消息(喊狼來了)，人們將不再相信你。
46. call off
取消
The meeting was called off because of the typhoon. 會議因為颱風而取消了。
47. carry on
繼續
Despite the noise, he carried on working. 儘管有噪音，他還是繼續工作。
48. catch out
識破；發覺(某人)犯錯
The teacher asked a tricky question to catch the students out. 老師問了一個刁鑽的問題來考倒學生(抓出沒懂的學生)。
49. cut off
切斷；中斷
Our electricity was cut off because we didn't pay the bill. 因為沒繳費，我們的電被切斷了。
50. compete for
為...競爭
Many athletes compete for the gold medal in the Olympic Games. 許多運動員在奧運中爭奪金牌。
