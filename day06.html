1. 單字字彙 (Vocabulary)

antique
n. 古董；古物 / adj. 古董的
My grandfather loves collecting antiques. 我祖父喜歡收集古董。
anonymous
adj. 匿名的；來源不明的
The donor wishes to remain anonymous. 捐贈者希望保持匿名。
apartment
n. 公寓
They live in a luxury apartment in downtown Taipei. 他們住在台北市中心的一間豪華公寓裡。
appliance
n. 家用電器
Modern household appliances have made housework much easier. 現代家用電器讓家事變得容易許多。
apparently
adv. 顯然地；據說
Apparently, he decided to leave the company for personal reasons. 顯然地，他因為個人因素決定離開公司。
approve
v. 贊成；批准
The committee approved the budget for the new project. 委員會批准了新專案的預算。
(approve of 贊成)
approach
v. 接近；著手處理 / n. 方法
The winter is approaching. 冬天即將來臨。
We need a new approach to solving this problem. 我們需要一個解決這個問題的新方法。
applaud
v. 鼓掌；讚許
The audience applauded loudly after the performance. 表演結束後，觀眾大聲鼓掌。
artist
n. 藝術家
She is a talented artist who specializes in oil painting. 她是一位擅長油畫的天才藝術家。
appearance
n. 外表；出現
Don't judge a person by their appearance. 不要以貌取人。

3. 搭配詞 (Collocations) - 範圍 p.64

請特別注意粗體字的介系詞或動詞搭配。

51. contrary to
與...相反
Contrary to popular belief, camels do not store water in their humps. 與普遍的看法相反，駱駝不把水儲存在駝峰裡。
52. count on
依靠；指望 (= rely on / depend on)
You can always count on me when you are in trouble. 當你有麻煩時，你可以隨時依靠我。
53. cut down
削減；砍倒
The doctor told him to cut down on smoking and drinking. 醫生告訴他要減少抽煙和喝酒。
54. deal with
處理；應付；涉及
This book deals with the history of the Roman Empire. 這本書涉及羅馬帝國的歷史。
55. depend on
依賴；視...而定
Our picnic depends on the weather. 我們的野餐視天氣而定。
56. deprive of
剝奪 (deprive sb. of sth.)
The new law deprives citizens of their right to protest. 新法律剝奪了公民抗議的權利。
57. die of
死於 (疾病、飢渴等內因)
Many people died of hunger during the war. 戰爭期間許多人死於飢餓。
(補: die from 死於意外、受傷等外因)
58. die out
滅絕；逐漸消失
Dinosaurs died out millions of years ago. 恐龍在數百萬年前就滅絕了。
59. do without
沒有...也行；將就；省去
There is no milk left, so we'll have to do without (it). 牛奶沒了，所以我們只能將就著不加了。
60. drive at
意指；用意何在 (通常用於進行式)
I don't understand what you are driving at. 我不懂你的用意何在 (你想表達什麼)。
